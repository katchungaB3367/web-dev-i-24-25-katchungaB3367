Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

If you don't have eggs, you can use applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt. Use 1/4 cup for each egg.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C.
  2. Mix the flour and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Cream the butter, sugars, and vanilla together.
  4. Add the eggs one by one and mix well.
  5. Add the dry mixture slowly into the wet mixture.
  6. Mix in the chocolate chips.
  7. Put small scoops of dough on the baking tray.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes until golden.
  9. Let the cookies cool before eating.
